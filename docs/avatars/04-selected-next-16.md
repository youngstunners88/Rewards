## Review rule

Keep only designs that are clearly cartoon, age-appropriate, original-looking, readable at thumbnail size and free from logos, readable text, real-team marks or close likenesses. If a variation fails any rule, do not assign it to a student; keep it only as a rejected-generation record or delete it.
